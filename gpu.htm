GPU Sales

Explore how we acquire our GPUs

  • Direct Purchases from Manufacturers: We have strong relationships with leading GPU manufacturers like Nvidia and AMD. This allows us to purchase GPUs directly from them, ensuring that we always have the latest models in stock.
  • Partnerships with Distributors: We also work with a network of trusted distributors to source GPUs. This helps us to maintain a diverse inventory and meet the varied needs of our customers.
  • Server Integrations: Some of our GPUs are sourced from complete servers made by Dell, Supermicro, and Gigabyte. These servers come integrated with Nvidia GPUs, providing us with another reliable source of high-quality GPUs.
  • Customer Trade-Ins: We offer a trade-in program where customers can exchange their old GPUs for store credit. This not only provides our customers with a cost-effective way to upgrade their GPUs, but also allows us to offer pre-owned GPUs at a lower price point.
